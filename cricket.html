üìö Cricket Market Framework

How Different Formats Create Different Edges

  • ODI markets behave like low-volatility equities ‚Äî predictable outcomes, favorites usually deliver, limited variance creates anchor bet opportunities
  • T20 markets behave like options ‚Äî variance is the asset class, favorites are systematically overpriced due to knockout structure punishing consistency
  • IPL outrights resemble league futures ‚Äî form + depth + system beats individual stars; franchise pedigree and playoff experience create structural edges
  • National bias premium ‚Äî home country markets (India especially) inflate domestic team pricing 3-5% above fair value due to retail patriot premium

üîç Understanding ArbScore in Cricket

What is ArbScore?

Proprietary metric combining market inefficiency, format-specific variance, and structural edge. Scores above 65 indicate strong value; below 40 suggests overpriced or efficient pricing.

Format Adjustments

ODI scores weighted toward stability (lower variance = lower score ceiling). T20 scores weighted toward convexity (high variance = higher score potential for non-favorites).

National Bias Detection

Indian team markets automatically receive -5 ArbScore adjustment for domestic retail premium. Other home nations receive -2 adjustment for local bias.

Tournament vs League

Knockout tournaments favor underdogs (higher ArbScore for 2nd/3rd tier). League formats favor consistency (higher ArbScore for system-driven franchises).
