About Arbstack

What this project is, what it is not, and why it exists.

What is Arbstack?

Arbstack is built around a simple idea: edges exist when markets misprice reality.

Most betting and prediction platforms encourage impulse decisions. Arbstack does the opposite.

  • Rank outcomes by expected edge, not hype
  • Surface uncertainty instead of hiding it
  • Separate signal from fan bias

If a position does not have positive expected value, Arbstack assumes it should not exist.

Philosophy

Inspired by probability theory, Kelly sizing, and market microstructure, Arbstack treats every decision as a trade-off between risk, reward, and survival.

This site is a static demo. No execution. No advice. No guarantees. Only ranked information.

What’s implemented

Static · JSON-driven
  • Unified layout across pages
  • Sports hub with ranked matchdays
