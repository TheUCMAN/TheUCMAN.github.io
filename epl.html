üí° What These Patterns Mean

1) Favorites often overpriced

Big teams like Man City, Arsenal, and Chelsea tend to get bet up too high on Kalshi compared to what bookies think. That's where the fade opportunities come from.

2) Draws are your friend

When fading a favorite, combining the underdog with a draw gives you two ways to win. It's like betting against the favorite without as much risk.

3) Look for 3%+ differences

Small differences might just be market noise. When you see 3% or more, that's when it gets interesting and worth considering.

4) Prices add up over 100%

When Home + Draw + Away totals over 105%, there's serious mispricing happening. These are the best spots to look for opportunities.

üéØ Simple Betting Strategies

  • Fade the Favorite: When a big team is overpriced (3%+ too high), bet NO on them or combine the Draw + Underdog.
  • Watch the Odds: If bookmaker odds move to match Kalshi, your edge is gone - time to reconsider.
  • Take Profits: If your bet moves 3-5 cents in your favor, consider cashing out. These edges don't last forever.
  • Live Hedging: If you bet against the favorite and they score first, you can buy a small amount of favorite YES to limit losses.
